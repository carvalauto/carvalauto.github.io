Guangzhou CarvalAuto part co,.ltd Founded in 2008, our company started as a professional auto parts store. With over a decade of deep cultivation in the automotive service industry, we have achieved a leap from a single auto parts retailer to a comprehensive automotive service provider, relying on solid technical expertise, rigorous service standards and an excellent market reputation. Today, we have become an influential one-stop automotive service brand in the Guangzhou and Foshan region.

After years of in-depth development and layout, our company has established a service network consisting of 6 self-operated auto repair shops and 2 directly-operated auto parts stores in the core areas of Guangzhou and Foshan, with a team of more than 80 professional technical and service personnel. Our business fully covers a full chain of automotive services including original-equivalent auto parts supply, vehicle repair of various models, routine maintenance, fault detection and care upgrades, providing professional, efficient and reliable one-stop vehicle solutions for individual car owners, corporate fleets and other customers.

In the core auto parts supply system, the company adheres to quality first and has established long-term and stable cooperation with a number of professional manufacturers. All auto parts strictly comply with 100 %original quality standards, featuring high cost performance and wide adaptability, covering many mainstream models on the market. More importantly, each auto part is first subjected to actual vehicle installation, road testing and long-term verification in our self-operated repair shops to ensure stable performance and safety, escorting customers' driving safety from the source.

In terms of service models, the company focused on Japanese and German models with high technical barriers at the initial stage, accumulating rich maintenance experience and auto parts inventory. In 2020, responding to the rapid rise of China's independent brand automotive market and changes in customer demand, the company took the initiative to expand its service scope, adding maintenance and auto parts supply services for mainstream domestic models such as Baojun, Wuling and Geely, and continuously improving its service matrix. With a more comprehensive model coverage and more professional technical capabilities, we meet the diversified vehicle needs of different customers.

In the future, the company will continue to uphold the business philosophy of "Ingenious Service, Quality First", constantly optimize the service network and improve technical levels, striving to become a benchmark enterprise of comprehensive automotive services trusted by customers and recognized by the industry in the region.
