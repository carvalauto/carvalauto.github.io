About Us

Who We Are

A trusted one-stop automotive service provider in Guangzhou and Foshan.

Guangzhou Carval Auto Parts Co., Ltd. was founded in 2008. After years of development, we have become a trusted one-stop automotive service provider in Guangzhou and Foshan.

We own 6 repair shops, 2 offices and 5 spare parts stores, supported by a professional team of over 80 members. We provide 100% original quality auto parts and full-range services including vehicle repair, maintenance and fault diagnosis.

All our parts strictly follow OEM standards and are verified by real-vehicle tests. Starting with Japanese, Korean, German models, we now also serve Baojun, Wuling, Geely and other mainstream Chinese brands.

With the philosophy of "Ingenious Service, Quality First", we are committed to becoming a leading benchmark in the regional automotive service industry.
